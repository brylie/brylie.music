From the album *Rational Limits*, "Anticipation" features textured chamber strings and woodwinds creating an atmosphere of expectant tension and forward motion.

## Musical Character

The piece builds gradually, with interweaving string and woodwind lines that suggest something emerging just beyond the horizon. The textured orchestration creates layers of meaning and emotional depth, capturing the complex feelings associated with anticipation - excitement, nervousness, hope, and uncertainty all mingling together.

This composition explores the emotional space of waiting for something significant to arrive or happen.
